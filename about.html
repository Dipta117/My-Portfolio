About Me

Hello! I'm Dipta, a passionate web developer currently pursuing my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering at KUET.

I love building interactive, user-friendly websites and bringing ideas to life through code. My journey into web development started from curiosity and quickly turned into a deep interest in front-end design, responsive layouts, and creative problem-solving.

I enjoy learning new technologies and constantly improving my skills. I work with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and ASP.NET. I'm also comfortable using tools like Git, GitHub, and Visual Studio Code.

Beyond coding, I'm curious about UI/UX design and accessibility, and I'm always exploring ways to make web experiences more engaging and inclusive. When I'm not in front of a screen, I enjoy music, photography, and discovering cool tech projects online. I'm excited to grow as a developer and work on meaningful projects that make a difference.
